About Me

I am a Teacher in Kavresthali Secondary School, Kavresthali, Kathmandu.

I have been teaching for several years and love helping students discover the joy of learning. My classes focus on building strong fundamentals, encouraging curiosity, and applying real-world examples.

In addition to classroom teaching, I mentor students on science projects, entrance exam preparation, and personal development. Outside of school, I enjoy reading, exploring new teaching techniques, and sharing knowledge through community workshops.
